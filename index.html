IMPORTANCE OF BIOMOLECULE

BIOMOLECULES


Biomolecules are crucial for life as it helps living beings to develop, support, and replicate.
They are engaged with building organic entities from single cells to complex living creatures like people, by connecting with one another.
The variety in their shape and construction gives variety in their capabilities.

    CARBOHYDRATE:

    Carbs are artificially characterized as polyhydroxy aldehydes or ketones or mixtures which produce them on hydrolysis.

    PROTIENS:

    Proteins are polymers of amino acids organized as polypeptide chains.Most catalysts are proteinaceous in nature.

    NUCLEIC ACID:

    Nucleic acids allude to the hereditary material tracked down in the cell that conveys all the genetic data from guardians to offspring.

    LIPIDS:

    Lipids are natural substances that are insoluble in water, dissolvable in natural solvents, are connected with unsaturated fats, and are used by the living cell.
